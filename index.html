What is YearUp?

Year Up's mission is to close the Opportunity Divide by ensuring that young adults gain the skills, experiences, and support that will empower them to reach their potential through careers and higher education.

Soon Learn About YearUp in a College Fair, I decided to join the next class, class 7 in the App Developer track. The Fisrt Six Months of YearUp were College credit classes(classes depened on what track you join) & YearUp classes that focused on training us to be ready for our upcoming Corparte Internship. The Second Six Months I was assigned a Techincal Writer in Salesforce, before my Internship ended I was given a job opportunity.
